# RevenueCat — Decision Record
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
## What it is                                                                                                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
RevenueCat is a subscription management SDK and dashboard that sits between the                                                                                                                                             
app and the two billing systems (Apple StoreKit / Google Play Billing). It                                                                                                                                                  
normalises both into one SDK and one webhook format.                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
**What it does for MindFlow:**                                                                                                                                                                                              
| Layer | What RevenueCat handles | Where it lives |                                                                                                                                                                        
|-------|------------------------|----------------|                                                                                                                                                                         
| Mobile | Product fetching, purchase flow, restore — wrapping StoreKit & Play Billing | `mobile/services/purchases.ts` (~70 lines) |                                                                                       
| Dashboard | Product/offering config, pricing, A/B tests — without app updates | external |                                                                                                                                
| Backend | Unified webhook events: `INITIAL_PURCHASE`, `RENEWAL`, `CANCELLATION`, `EXPIRATION`, `BILLING_ISSUE` | `backend/internal/revenuecat/` (~150 lines) |                                                            
| DB write | Translates events to `subscription_type` + `subscription_expires_at` on `users` | `repository.go` (one UPDATE query) |                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
The backend webhook handler logic is: when RevenueCat says the user                                                                                                                                                         
bought/renewed → set tier to monthly/yearly; when cancelled/expired → set to                                                                                                                                                
free.                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
## Why it was chosen
The alternative is implementing two separate billing stacks:                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
- **iOS**: StoreKit 2 (Swift, needs RN bridging) + App Store Server                                                                                                                                                         
   Notifications v2 (JWS-signed payloads, Apple root cert verification)                                                                                                                                                      
- **Android**: Google Play Billing Library 6+ + Real-time developer                                                                                                                                                         
   notifications via Google Cloud Pub/Sub                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
Getting either right is roughly a week of work. Getting both right — including                                                                                                                                              
edge cases like grace periods, billing retries, offer redemptions, family                                                                                                                                                   
sharing — is a month-plus project. RevenueCat replaces all of that with ~220                                                                                                                                                
lines of code.                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
## Pricing                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
Based on **MTR (Monthly Tracked Revenue)** — gross subscription revenue                                                                                                                                                     
flowing through it:                                                                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
| Tier | Cost | Threshold |                                                                                                                                                                                                 
|------|------|-----------|                                                                                                                                                                                                 
| Free | $0 | Up to $2,500 MTR/month |                                                                                                                                                                                      
| Starter | ~$99/month flat | ~$2.5k–$10k MTR |                                                                                                                                                                             
| Pro | ~1% of MTR | ~$10k–$50k MTR |                                                                                                                                                                                       
| Business | ~0.75% of MTR | $50k+ MTR |                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
At launch MindFlow will almost certainly sit on the free tier — it covers the                                                                                                                                               
first ~250 monthly subscribers ($9.99) or ~125 annual ($19.99).                                                                                                                                                              
> Verify current pricing at revenuecat.com/pricing before financial planning;                                                                                                                                               
> tiers may shift.                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
## How hard it would be to remove                 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
**Code coupling is low.** Everything RevenueCat touches is self-contained:                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
- Delete `mobile/services/purchases.ts` and `backend/internal/revenuecat/` —                                                                                                                                                
   that is the entire integration.                                                                                                                                                                                           
- The DB schema (`subscription_type`, `subscription_expires_at`) is unchanged;                                                                                                                                              
  any replacement would write the same fields.                                                                                                                                                                              
- `app/paywall.tsx` calls 4 functions from `purchases.ts`; swap them for the                                                                                                                                                
   replacement SDK's equivalents.                                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
 **The effort is not deleting RevenueCat — it is what replaces it:**                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
 1. **Mobile**: No mature, production-grade unified React Native IAP library                                                                                                                                                 
    exists at RevenueCat's level of polish. `expo-iap` (formerly                                                                                                                                                             
    `react-native-iap`) is the main alternative but has a history of edge-case                                                                                                                                               
    bugs and less active maintenance.  
 2. **Backend**: The webhook handler would be replaced by two separate parsers —                                                                                                                                             
    Apple's JWS-signed server notifications (certificate chain verification) and                                                                                                                                             
    Google's Pub/Sub messages. The logic is similar but both formats are more                                                                                                                                                
    complex than RevenueCat's normalised payload.                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
 3. **Offering management**: Pricing changes and free-trial additions currently                                                                                                                                              
    require no app update. DIY means shipping a release for each change.                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
 **Rough migration effort:** 2–4 weeks for a developer familiar with both                                                                                                                                                    
 platforms (~€4k–€10k+ at freelance rates).                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
 **Break-even:** RevenueCat becomes more expensive than the migration cost at                                                                                                                                                
 roughly $40k–$50k MTR ($400–$500/month in fees). Below that revenue level it                                                                                                                                                
 almost never makes financial sense to leave.                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
 **Summary:** the integration is thin and reversible, but practically you would                                                                                                                                              
 only migrate at significant revenue — and at that point the fee is a small                                                                                                                                                  
 fraction of earnings. The main risk to watch is a RevenueCat pricing change;                                                                                                                                                
 the generous free tier makes it low-risk for early-stage.
